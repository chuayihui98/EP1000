Key Points when using Fab Lab

1. Appropriate Clothing: Long covered pants, covered shoe with rubber soles and no accessories.

2. Long hair must be tied.

3. Be alert of your surroundings.

4. No food and drinks allowed.

5. Seek permission from the Technical Supporting Officer or Lecturer before operating the machines in Fab Lab.

6. Report any faulty machines or emergencies to the Technical Supporting Officer or Lecturer present.

7. Wear safety gears when required.
