Conclusion:

I am satisfied with the result of my prototype even though there are room for improvements. I could've made changes to the measurement in the overall design for my arduino board to fit in better. As for 3D printing, I could've printed something for the inner part of the light box to give it a depth of field aesthetic. Using red LED light to enhance the design of the sharingan would be more effective than using a white LED light.
